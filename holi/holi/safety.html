🌸 Holi Safety Tips 🌸

Enjoy Holi Safely with These Important Guidelines

🎨 Celebrate Holi Responsibly

Holi is a festival of joy, but it's essential to take precautions to keep yourself and others safe while enjoying the festival.

⚠️ General Safety Tips

🌿 Use Natural Colors

Choose **organic and herbal colors** to protect your skin and avoid chemicals.

👓 Protect Your Eyes

Wear **sunglasses** to prevent color from getting into your eyes.

🥤 Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and juices to stay refreshed during celebrations.

💧 Apply Oil on Skin & Hair

Oil your skin and hair to avoid deep color absorption.

👶🐶 Safety Tips for Kids & Pets

🧒 Supervise Children

Ensure kids play in a safe area with **skin-friendly colors**.

🐾 Keep Pets Away

Colors can be toxic for animals. **Avoid applying colors to pets**.

🌍 Eco-Friendly Holi

Protect nature by reducing water usage and playing dry Holi.

  • Use **biodegradable colors**
  • Avoid **wasting water**
  • Celebrate **responsibly** with family & friends
