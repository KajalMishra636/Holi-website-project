Ancient Origins

Holi dates back to ancient Hindu traditions, with references in scriptures like the Puranas.

Evolution Over Time

From traditional bonfires to modern-day celebrations, Holi has transformed through the centuries.

Holi Across Regions

Holi is celebrated differently in Mathura, Barsana, Vrindavan, and other parts of India.

Ways People Celebrate Holi

Lathmar Holi

A unique tradition from Barsana where women playfully beat men with sticks.

Traditional Holi

People gather to throw colors, dance, and sing, celebrating the festival with joy.

Eco-friendly Holi

Many are now opting for organic colors and waterless Holi to preserve the environment.

Holi Safety Tips

Skin Protection

Apply coconut oil to your skin before playing Holi to prevent color absorption.

Eye Care

Avoid contact with harsh chemicals and wear protective eyewear for safety.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and avoid excessive consumption of intoxicating drinks like bhang.
